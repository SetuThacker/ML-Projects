So, Let's get Started,
 

1st Degree Connections means >

X is on profile of Y,
X was seen there "1st degree" that means,
X & Y are friend, like Facebook friends.
 
2nd Degree Connections means >
X is viewing the profile of Z,
X was seen there 2nd Degree that means,
X has on one friend whose name is Y. Y and Z are friend like X & Y.
So X has relation with Z via Y .
This is known as 2nd Degree Connection.
 
3rd Degree Connections means >   
As per Above,
X has relation with Z.
A is friend of Z ( like X with Y )then,
X would seen that "3rd Degree" connection.
 
It is very Confusing to Understand, but by the time you will get to know.
 
3rd+ connections means >
As you have seen X has 3rd Degree connection with A.
A & B are friend like X & Y.
B has Only 1 friend whose name is A.
